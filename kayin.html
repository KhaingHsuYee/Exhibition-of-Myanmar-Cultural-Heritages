Kayin State

Best Places to Visit in Kayin State

Kayin State, also known by the endonyms Kawthoolei and Karen State, is a state of Myanmar. The capital city is Hpa-An, also spelled Pa-An. The relief of Karen State is mountainous with the Dawna Range running along the state in a NNW - SSE direction and the southern end of the Karen Hills in the northwest.Some Famous Places you should visit once in a lifetime in Kayin State are as follows.

1.Mount Zwegabin,Hpa An

The Mount Zwegabin is one of the well-known mountains in Myanmar and people always regard and memorize Hpa An with the Mount Zwegabin. It takes two or four hours to reach and see the monastery, pagoda at the top of the mountain and the entrance fee is around 3000kyats at the entrance to the mountain.

2.Sadan Cave(Hpa An)

There is a famous cave called Sadan Cave with full of Buddha images and pagodas in the ways of the cave. As the paintings are rare and historical arts, visitors will feel pleasant because of these paintings and the convenient environment in the cave.

3.Kayin State cultural Museum

Kayin state cultural Museum is located in Hpa An and there are beautiful displays with Buddhaâ€™s images, Kayin cultures, ivories, literature, and Kayin traditional musical instruments. The entrance fee is around 2 USD.

4.Kyauk Ka Lat Pagoda

A visit to Kyauk Ka Lat Pagoda offers the opportunity to reflect on the mesmerizing architecture while escaping the noise and the city rush. Occupying a secluded spot in the middle of a lake, the pagoda can be reached by a relaxing kayak ride. The pagoda, situated on top of a rock, represents an important religious building; if you join a tour, you'll learn a bit about the local history and religious beliefs. Take some time to reflect and enjoy the view of the scenic landscape, with the lake and the hills in the background.

5.Kyone Htaw waterfall

Kyone Htaw waterfall is next to Htiklawtho and is located at HoaingBwe town in Kayin State. KyoneHtaw waterfall is surrounded by a pristine forest. The waterfall is becoming a popular attraction for both local and forgein tourists who are drawn by the naturally green-coloured water. The best time to visit is at the drier times of the year.

6.Kan Thar Yar Lake

Kan Thar Yar Lake, lying just on the south side of Hpa An, is absolutely an ideal destination for those who are eager to keep away from an urban life that makes you feel suffocated in order to look for a little change of scenery.

7.Naw Bu Baw Prayer Mountain

Naw Bu Baw Prayer Mountain is in the heart of Kayin State. Easy to reach from Taungoo about 27 miles. The weather is so cool in summer and winter. I will not recommend you to visit in the rainy season. It is located at the Thandaunggyi and there have several guest house. You can catch the amazing sunrise from the mountain if you visit one night stay. It's really really BEAUTIFUL ! I promise you will not regret.

8.Bat Cave

Bat Cave, one of the must-see destination in Hpa An, the home of an endless stream of over 100,000s of bats, will sure give adventurous visitors immense satisfaction.Just 9 km away from the city, near Thanlyin river, Hpa-an, Kayin state, Bat Cave is a small cave that cannot be entered. Nevertheless, tourists can absolutely go there when hundreds of thousands of bats flying out of their cave at sunset. When the bats start flying out, they start beating drums so that the bats make interesting patterns in flight not only for five minutes but at least 40 minutes. What a wonderful nature thing!
