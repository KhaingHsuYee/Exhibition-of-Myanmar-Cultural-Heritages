Popular Destinations

Kachin State

Kachin State is a beautiful state located nothern of Myanmar and is adorned by the king of mountains,the Himalayas.Hkakabo Razi,the highest peak of Myanmar is also located here.

Shan State

Shan State has been quoted by many a famous traveller as the land of picture postard settings.Occupying close to a quarter of Myanmar's land mass,it is often hard to find words to do justice to its beautiful scenery.

Kayin State

The relief of Karen State is mountainous with the Dawna Range running along the state in a NNW - SSE direction and the southern end of the Karen Hills in the northwest.

Rakhine State

Situated on the western coast, it is bordered by Chin State to the north, Magway Region, Bago Region and Ayeyarwady Region to the east, the Bay of Bengalto the west

Yangon(The old capital) offers you the most beautiful scenes

Yangon is often the first stop for foreigners visiting Myanmar.It is the former capital,the most populous city,and the largest commercial hub in the country.It's also the spiritual heart of Myanmar,being home to the Shwedagon Pagoda,regarded as the most sacred Buddhist pagoda among Burmese.

Mandalay(The Old Ancient Kingdom Capital) will Show you the Rich Culture

Mandalay is the second-largest city in Myanmar,after Yangon.Mandalay was founded in 1857 by King Mindon,replacing Amarapura as the new royal capital of the Konbaung dynasty.It was Burma's final royal capital before the kingdom's annexation by the British Empire in 1885.Under British rule,Mandalay remained commercially and culturally important despite the rise of Yangon,the new capital of British Burma.

At Bagan("The Land of Pagoda"),you will see a sea of temples

There are two preeminent ancient religious cities in Southeast Asia:Bagan in Burma and Angkor in Cambodia.Both sites are notable for their expanse of sacred geography and the number and size of their individual temples.For many visitors, Bagan is the more extraordinary because of its wonderful views.This temple is one of Myanmar's main attractions.Bagan that is commonly termed as a sea of temples by people across the world is an ancient city and a UNESCO World Heritage Site in the Mandalay Region of Myanmar.

Pyin Oo Lwin("The City of Flowers") is a beautiful city in Central Myanmar

Pyin Oo Lwin will take you back to 19th century Britain with its colonial red-coloured buildings and horse-driven carriages.owever,the buildings are not like the colonial buildings of Yangon and Mandalay.They are cute brick structures that will surely win your heart.
