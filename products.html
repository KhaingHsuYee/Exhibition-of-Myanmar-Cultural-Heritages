Myanmar's Products

Why should you buy Myanmar's Local Products...

After experiencing the travel throughout Myanmar, there are many things you should buy for souvenirs.Myanmar is not only a worth place to travel but also a great place to buy souvenirs...There are many arts and crafts, some real gems and jewelleries to be found in the markets, shops and factories."The crafts practiced in Burma are centuries old and many of them make great gifts and a way to support the Burmese culture."
Many of these hadicrafted items are available at local markets and artisan workshops.
