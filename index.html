About Myanmar

Myanmar, also called Burma, is a country, located in the western portion of mainland Southeast Asia.It is an impressive list of culture and traditions that mark the life of the Myanmarese. The months are packed with exciting festivals, delicious food, and beautiful traditional crafts.The country is home to important religious sites for Buddhists worldwide. Experience the spiritual and the supernatural in temples, pagodas and monasteries across the lush landscape.Flush with lush forests decorating green mountains, and magnificent bodies of water teeming with endemic species, the country is perfect for nature lovers.More than its rich recent historical past, Myanmar is home to the ancient Pyu cities as well as rare archaeological finds dating back to the Neolithic period.
