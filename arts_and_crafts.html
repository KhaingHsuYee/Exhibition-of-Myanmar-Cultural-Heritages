Myanmar's Arts and Crafts

About Myanmar's Arts and Crafts

Myanmar's religious and deeply traditional culture has contributed to a thriving arts and crafts scene for many centuries.
Myanmar Arts and Crafts reflect a rich culture, arts and architectures of the country.

There are ten best well-known traditional arts and crafts called "Pan Sel Myo" (10 types of Flowers).They are:

  1. Panchi(Paintings)
  2. Panpu
  3. Panbe
  4. Panyun
  5. Panpoot
  6. Panyan
  7. Pantamaut
  8. Patain
  9. Padte
  10. Pantaut

1.Lacquareware

Lacquareware is a special kind of handicrafts(the art of Panyun) ,which produce materials made of bamboo and polishing with a thick black varnish("sis-se" in Myanmar language). Traditional Myanmar lacquaware is "Yun Htae",one of the country's most famous handicrafts ad has a very unique style.
It has been existed since ancient Bagan dynasty and so this art work has a long tradition.

Some of the still remained ancient Lacquareware

These lacquare are still used and have different kinds of items. Typically,the colors are red,blue and green-it is useful to be applied to anything from bracelets, rings and necklaces to boxes,trays and furniture. In addition, food bowls, bowls for monks, lacquerware vessels, drinking cups and varios types of boxes can be seen as 'Laquare Products'.

2.Panpoot

The another still alive artwork is Panpoot,which is Myanmar's traditional sculpture. It has endured the long story since before the Bagan period.It is the art of making materials by rubbing and turning woods on the lathe turner .Wood,teak, stone and plaster as sculpting materials are employed.Much of Myanmar's sculpture is based in Buddhism and decorating with exquisite wood carvings can still be seen at some ancient monasteries and pagodas.

P.S: Now, Myanmar's wood carving industry has grown from being found exclusively in temples to being sold sold in shoppings centers and art galleries across the country. Even in traditional carved puppets and even coconuts.

3.Puppet

In the aspect of traditional performance, 'Yote Thay'(marionette puppetry) is related to the critical role.The Myanmar puppets are distinctive in dress style and the intricacy of manipulating them.The dolls usually have 20 strings, but some have as many as 60, giving the puppeteer groom to emulate uncanny dance movements.It shows the pride of Myanmar Traditional Dance and Culture and the standard of Art of Myanmar. In ancient times, the puppets operas are presented for Royal Puppets are about 2 feet tall and they are up with 60 lines.Until now, puppet shows are held, especially in Bagan, where tourists can see the real heritage of Myanmar.

There are many characters of puppets such as nat(deva),zawgyi,a king and queen, four ministers, a price and princess,nhat kadaw(spirit medium),bhiulu(ogre) ,animals, gods and emperors,etc.,and it'type of cute and good souvenir for e presents.You can see and buy puppet dolls across Myanmar, especially in Yangon, and Mandalay.
